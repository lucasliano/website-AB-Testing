About OpenRF Instruments

We are RF and microwave engineers building the instruments we wanted in our own labs: open, well-documented, and precise enough for serious work in industry and academia.

Engineering first

Our background spans RF front-end design, test instrumentation, and academic research. We validate performance against established lab equipment and document the results openly.

Committed to open standards

We favor open data formats, well-defined APIs, and transparent measurement workflows so you are never locked into a black box.

Long-term support

We actively maintain firmware, documentation, and example code, and use the same instruments in our own labs, ensuring issues are found and fixed quickly.

Our motivation

High-performance RF test equipment is often closed, expensive, and difficult to integrate with modern software workflows. We believe open hardware and accessible APIs can change that without compromising on performance.

Working with universities

We collaborate with educators to align lab exercises with real course objectives and to support long-term lab deployments, including volume pricing and support contracts.
